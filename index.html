About Me

I am a first-year Ph.D. student (2024) in Computer Science at the University of Queensland, advised by Dr. Mahsa Baktashmotlagh. My research focuses on the transferability and generalization of ML models. Before joining UQ, I earned a Master's degree in Computer Engineering (Graduate 2024) and a Bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering (Graduate 2021) from Sharif University of Technology. With over three years of experience (2021-2024) in the data science department at Tapsi, one of Iran's leading ride-hailing companies, I have applied my expertise in real-world business settings. I am passionate about advancing the field of ML and its practical applications.
